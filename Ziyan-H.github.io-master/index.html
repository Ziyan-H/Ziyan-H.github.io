Bio

I am currently a Master's candidate in Mathematics at Central South University, supervised by Prof. Hongqiao Wang. I received my Bachelor's degree in Information and Computing Science from Hohai University in 2024.

My research interests lie in computational imaging, imaging science, deep learning, and uncertainty quantification. I have hands-on experience in deep learning projects, including downstream tasks and fine-tuning Large Language Models. My expertise extends to developing self-supervised learning methods for solving ill-posed imaging inverse problems, and leveraging statistical tools like Bayesian Neural Networks and Gaussian Process Regression for robust uncertainty quantification. My research goal is to integrate mathematical theory with artificial intelligence to address key challenges in imaging problems in an interpretable manner.

Now，I have a strong research interest in 3D/4D reconstruction, human pose estimation, and embodied intelligence, and am committed to pursuing advanced research in these interdisciplinary areas.

I'm searching for a visiting student position now for 3D/4D reconstruction relevant areas.
